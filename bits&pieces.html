role:

graphic designer, brand marketer

objective:

create a welcoming t-shirt design for an all-women's group displaying a sense of openness and working together. the women's group had a pre-existing mascot, the pigglet, and the color scheme of pink and light blue.

outcome:

i was not only successfull in creating a design that the client loved, i was also able to produce all neccessary files with which to print onto t-shirts. understanding the production and printing process of designs, as well as the limitations of most printers, i created a packet of pre-sized, transparent vector images to send to the vendor.
